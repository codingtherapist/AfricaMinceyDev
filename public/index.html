Today I’ve been thinking about something I’ve thought about a lot: Semantic HTML. I know, kind of boring, but hear me out! It has come up recently in conversations in digital spaces. Web developers are asking themselves, does HTML semantics really matter? I’d like to toss my reply in the ring: HTML semantics ABSOLUTELY matter. It is often the thing stopping users from being able to use your website. I am so passionate about this because I have seen firsthand how users of assistive technology, which rely on reading HTML to give information to the user, are impacted.
